# Code of Conduct

This applies to issues, pull requests, and discussions on this repository.

Be respectful. No harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
Disagree with ideas, not people. Assume good faith.

The maintainer has final say on what violates this and how to respond
(warning, comment removal, or ban from the repo), proportionate to the
issue.
